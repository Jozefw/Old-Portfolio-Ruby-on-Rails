Play Pal

Play Pal is a Ruby on Rails App with Javasctipt add ons. Play Pal is a study in Entity Relationship Diagrams - how database information relates to each other. Play Pal brings people together who want to promote an event.

DEMO

Garage Guy App

Garage Guy is a Ruby on Rails App with both Javascript and Jquery add ons. Garage Guy allows users to create virtual Garage Sales where people may purchase items online without having to physically attend the sale.
